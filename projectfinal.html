General Features a Travel Website Should Have:
1. User-friendly Interface: Easy navigation with clear options for booking flights, hotels, and holiday packages.
2. Search & Compare: Tools to compare flight and hotel prices, ensuring the user gets the best deal.
3. Customizable Packages: Allow users to personalize their travel itineraries, offering flexibility with accommodation, sightseeing, and transport.
4. Reviews and Ratings: Display customer reviews and ratings for travel services, accommodations, and activities.
5. 24/7 Customer Support: A robust customer service system, offering support via phone, email, or live chat.
6. Deals & Discounts: Regular promotions, exclusive discounts, and reward programs like memberships to encourage loyalty.
7. Multi-device Compatibility: Ensure seamless functionality across mobile, desktop, and tablet devices.
8. Secure Payment Gateway: Provide a secure and versatile payment system supporting various methods like credit cards, net banking, and wallets.
